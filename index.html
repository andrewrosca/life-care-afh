About Life Care

Care That Feels Like Coming Home

At Life Care Adult Family Home, we believe the best care happens in a real home — not a large facility. Located at 4600 NE 126th Ave in Vancouver, WA, our home is exactly that: a warm, welcoming space where your loved one is known, valued, and cared for every day.

Founded in 2000 and operated by Estera, Cornel, and Deborah Rosca, Life Care has been a trusted home for Vancouver families for over 25 years. We accept a maximum of six residents — so your family member isn't just a room number, they're a person we know deeply, care for personally, and treat like our own.

As a family ourselves, we understand what it means to entrust the care of someone you love. We are fully licensed by the Washington State Department of Social and Health Services (DSHS), specializing in mental health care, dementia & memory loss, and memory loss.

What We Offer

Everything Your Loved One Needs

From daily personal care to specialized support — every service is tailored to each resident and delivered with warmth, skill, and genuine attention.

Mental Health Care

Structured, compassionate support for adults living with depression, anxiety, schizophrenia, bipolar disorder, and other mental health conditions — in a calm, home-like setting.

Dementia & Memory Care

Gentle, structured care for all stages of Alzheimer's and dementia. We create familiar routines, reduce anxiety, and focus on comfort and preserved dignity every single day.

Personal Care Assistance

Dignified, respectful help with bathing, grooming, dressing, mobility, and hygiene — all provided with patience, sensitivity, and genuine care.

Medication Management

Safe, accurate medication administration by trained caregivers following physician plans, with careful monitoring and coordination with each resident's healthcare team.

Nutritious Home Meals

Three home-cooked, well-balanced meals daily plus snacks — accommodating dietary needs, medical requirements, and personal food preferences for every resident.

Hospice & End-of-Life Care

Compassionate end-of-life care in a peaceful, familiar home. We partner with hospice providers to ensure comfort, dignity, and presence for residents and families.

Health Monitoring

Ongoing monitoring of vital signs and health changes, with prompt communication to physicians and specialists when needed.

Activities & Social Life

Enriching daily activities, gentle exercise, social time, and therapeutic recreation — designed to promote well-being, purpose, and genuine happiness.

Transportation & Appointments

We coordinate and assist with transportation to medical appointments, specialist visits, and essential outings — so your loved one never misses important care.

Everything families want to know about adult family home care in Vancouver, WA.

An adult family home (AFH) is a licensed residential home — not a nursing facility — that provides care for up to six adults. Life Care offers room, board, 24/7 supervision, personal care, medication management, and individualized support in a true home environment in Vancouver, WA.

We specialize in mental health care (depression, anxiety, schizophrenia, bipolar disorder) and dementia & Alzheimer's memory care. We also provide assisted living, hospice care, medication management, and personal care.

We accept a maximum of six residents, as required by Washington State licensing. This small size is intentional — it means every resident receives genuinely personal, one-on-one attention from our caregivers every single day.
